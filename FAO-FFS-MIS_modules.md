# FAO-FFS-MIS System Modules

## Core System Modules

### 1. E-Ledger Module
**Purpose:** Digital financial management system for Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLA)

- **Members** - Manage VSLA group members, their profiles, and membership status
- **Cycles** - Track savings cycles with start/end dates, cycle periods, and cycle completion
- **Shares** - Group settings. Record member share purchases, share values, and ownership stakes
- **Transactions** - Log all financial activities including deposits, withdrawals, and transfers
- **Loans** - Process loan applications, approvals, disbursements, and loan terms
- **Loan Payments** - Track loan repayments, interest calculations, and payment schedules
- **Statistics** - Generate financial reports, analytics, and performance metrics
- **Shakeouts** - Manage cycle-end distributions, profit sharing, and fund reallocation
- **Person Wallet** - Individual member digital wallets for storing funds
- **Wallet Transactions** - Record all wallet-based financial movements and balances
- **Withdraws** - Process member fund withdrawals and withdrawal requests

---

### 2. Advisory Module
**Purpose:** Agricultural knowledge sharing and farmer support system

- **Articles/Blog** - Educational content management with multiple formats:
  - **Phenology** - Crop growth stages, timing, and development cycles
  - **Rich Text** - Written articles, guides, and farming best practices
  - **Audios** - Voice recordings for farmer education and advisory content
  - **Videos** - Visual tutorials, demonstrations, and training materials
  - **PDF** - Downloadable guides, manuals, and reference documents
- **Farmer Questions & Answers** - Interactive Q&A forum where farmers can ask questions and receive answers from experts, extension officers, and fellow farmers
- **Manya AI Chatbot** - Intelligent virtual assistant for instant farming advice, troubleshooting, and information retrieval
- **Weather Forecast** - Real-time weather data, predictions, and climate information for farm planning

---

### 3. Marketplace Module
**Purpose:** Digital platform for agricultural commerce and service connections

- **Products and Services Posting** - Allow farmers and vendors to list agricultural products, inputs, and services
- **Products Listing** - Browse and search available agricultural products with pricing and availability
- **Service Providers Listing** - Directory of agricultural service providers (equipment rental, processing, transportation)
- **Look for the Product** - Advanced search functionality with filters for products, location, price, and quality
- **Products Pricings** - Real-time market prices, price trends, and comparative pricing information

---

### 4. Profiling Module
**Purpose:** User registration and stakeholder management system

- **Register Groups** - Onboard and manage Farmer Field School groups, VSLA groups, and cooperatives
- **Register System Users** - Multi-stakeholder user registration including:
  - **Service Providers** - Extension workers, agronomists, veterinarians, and consultants
  - **Input Dealers** - Seed suppliers, fertilizer dealers, pesticide vendors
  - **Farm Equipment Service Providers** - Tractor services, irrigation equipment, processing machinery
  - **Output Markets** - Buyers, processors, exporters, and market aggregators
  - **Aggregators** - Collection centers, bulking points, and wholesale buyers
  - **Bulking Agents** - Intermediaries who consolidate farmer produce for market access
- **Farmers** - Individual farmer registration with farm details, crops grown, and production data

---

### 5. FFS Activities Module
**Purpose:** Farmer Field School program management and monitoring

- **Trainings** - Schedule, conduct, and track FFS training sessions and curricula
- **Events & Scheduled Trainings** - Calendar management for upcoming events, workshops, and learning sessions
- **GAP (Good Agricultural Practices)** - Record and monitor adoption of recommended farming practices
- **FFS AESA Monitor (Stats)** - Agro-Ecosystem Analysis monitoring with field observations, pest/disease tracking, and ecological assessments

---

## Quick Actions
**Purpose:** Rapid access shortcuts for common administrative tasks

- **Register a User** - Fast-track user onboarding for farmers, service providers, or stakeholders
- **Register Group** - Quick group creation for FFS groups or VSLA associations
- **Create Savings Cycle** - Initiate new savings cycle for active groups
- **Record Transaction** - Quick entry for financial transactions and activities
- **Post Advisory Content** - Rapid publishing of urgent farming advisories or alerts
- **Report Issue** - Fast reporting mechanism for system issues or farmer problems

---

## Module Integration Points

- **E-Ledger ↔ Profiling**: Member profiles linked to financial accounts
- **Advisory ↔ Marketplace**: Product recommendations based on advisory content
- **FFS Activities ↔ Advisory**: Training materials integrated with knowledge base
- **Marketplace ↔ Profiling**: Service provider verification and farmer buyer connections
- **E-Ledger ↔ Marketplace**: Payment processing for product purchases

---

## System Features Across All Modules

- **Mobile-First Design** - Optimized for smartphone access in rural areas
- **Offline Capability** - Local data storage with sync when connectivity available
- **Multi-Language Support** - English, local languages, and voice interface
- **Role-Based Access Control** - Different permissions for farmers, admins, extension workers
- **Real-Time Notifications** - Push alerts for payments, trainings, market opportunities
- **Data Analytics** - Comprehensive reporting across all modules
- **Audit Trail** - Complete activity logging for transparency and accountability
